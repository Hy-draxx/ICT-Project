INTRODUCTION

The Information Communication Technology (ICT) centre at IBBUL is as old as the University itself. The unit is created to render information technology services to staff and students of the university.
Our mandate is to provide ICT-based training, facilities, and support services that will empower members of the University community to employ the use of ICT in their day-to-day activities. The unit is equipped with skilled manpower and emerging technologies to allow the University take a pride position as one of the best ICT- driven institutions in Nigeria and the world at large.

MISSION

Provision of state of the art ICT infrastructure and services for enhancing teaching, learning, and community services towards producing globally competitive graduates.



VISION

To be a reputable centre with state-of-the-art infrastructure and highly skilled manpower to prop up research, teaching, learning and hands on services in the university and community at large.

Services Rendered

IBBUL ICT is developing to being the backbone to the university’s day-to-day operations with services like:

  1. Provision of Internet services
  2. Email services
  3. Web services (Managing the university’s website and portals)
  4. Software application development
  5. E-learning
  6. Troubleshoot computer hardware
