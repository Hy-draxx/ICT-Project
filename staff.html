Message From The Director

Our mandate is to provide ICT-based training, facilities, and support services that will empower members of the University community to employ the use of ICT in their day-to-day activities. The unit is equipped with skilled manpower and emerging technologies to allow the University take a pride position as one of the best ICT- driven institutions in Nigeria and the world at large.
